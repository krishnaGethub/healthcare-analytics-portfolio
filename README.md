## 📁 Projects

### 🔴 Project 1: Healthcare Claims Denial Analysis
**File:** `project1_claims_denial_analysis.sql`

**Business Problem:** Identify why claims are getting denied and where revenue is leaking.

**What I built:**
- Analyzed claims data for denial patterns by payer and reason
- Identified top denial codes: CO-15, CO-18, CO-97, CO-4
- Calculated payer-wise denial rates and collection rates
- Built AR aging bucket analysis (0-30, 31-60, 61-90, 90+ days)
- Used Window Functions: RANK, SUM OVER, AVG OVER

**Key Results:**
- Missing prior auth (CO-15) caused 40% of all denials
- Medicare showed highest denial rate at 60%
- AR over 90 days identified as high bad-debt risk
